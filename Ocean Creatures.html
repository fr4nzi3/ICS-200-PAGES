Blue Whales

Blue Whales are a marine mammal and a baleen whale. With a maximum verified length of 29.9 meters and a maximum weight of 199 tonnes, this creature is the biggest mammal ever recorded in history. The long, thin body of a blue whale can have several dorsal tints of greyish-blue and differ somewhat in color beneath.

Dolphins

Dolphins are clever, nimble, gregarious, playful, cheerful, and sharing many emotional traits with humans. Dolphins come in an amazing variety of species, and each one has its own distinct personality and traits!
